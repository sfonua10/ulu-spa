# Group Package Images

This directory contains lifestyle photography for the group booking packages on the ULU Head Spa website.

## Image Requirements

All images should be **high-quality lifestyle photography** that conveys luxury, relaxation, and the specific group dynamic. Images should feel aspirational and authentic.

### Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or WebP (for best compression)
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1200x800px (3:2 aspect ratio recommended)
- **Orientation**: Landscape
- **File size**: Optimize to under 200KB for web performance
- **Style**: Natural lighting, warm tones, soft focus, luxury spa aesthetic

### Required Images

#### 1. `couples.jpg`
**Package**: Couples Retreat
**Concept**: Intimate spa experience for two people
**Suggested imagery**:
- Two people in spa robes, relaxed and happy
- Side-by-side spa beds or chairs
- Romantic, serene atmosphere
- Soft candlelight or natural light
- Warm, intimate setting

**Mood**: Romantic, peaceful, connected

## Color Grading Guidelines

To maintain brand consistency, consider applying these adjustments to all images:

1. **Slight gold/warm tint** to align with ULU's gold accent color (#B89752)
2. **Soft vignette** around edges for focus and luxury feel
3. **Increased warmth** (+10-15%) in color temperature
4. **Gentle desaturation** of blues/cyans to enhance sage/gold tones
5. **Subtle glow/soft focus** for dreamy, luxurious aesthetic
